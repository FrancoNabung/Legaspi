Oral Prophylaxis

Price: 700Php

Oral prophylaxis, or dental cleaning, maintains oral health by removing plaque, tartar, and stains. A dentist or hygienist examines your mouth, scales the teeth to remove buildup, and polishes them to prevent plaque accumulation. An optional fluoride treatment strengthens enamel. Regular cleanings, recommended twice a year, help keep teeth and gums healthy.

Tooth Restoration

Price: 900Php

Tooth restoration refers to various dental procedures aimed at repairing or replacing damaged or missing teeth to restore their function and appearance. These procedures can include fillings, crowns, bridges, implants, and dentures. The goal of tooth restoration is to ensure the health and structural integrity of the teeth, improve aesthetics, and enhance overall oral health.

Tooth Extraction

Price: 800Php

Tooth extraction, performed by a dentist or oral surgeon, involves removing a tooth from its socket due to severe decay, infection, crowding, or trauma. Simple extractions remove visible teeth with local anesthesia, while surgical extractions, used for broken or impacted teeth, require incisions and sometimes bone removal. Proper post-extraction care, including pain management and keeping the area clean, is essential for healing and preventing infection.

Removable Denture

Price: 8,00Php

A removable denture is a dental appliance designed to replace missing teeth, which can be taken out and put back by the patient. There are two main types: partial dentures for some missing teeth and complete dentures for all missing teeth. They restore teeth function and appearance, aiding in chewing, speaking, and preventing facial structure changes due to tooth loss.

Orthodontic Braces

Price: 6,000Php

Orthodontic braces correct misaligned teeth and jaws, improving dental health and the appearance of a smile. They use brackets, an archwire, and elastic bands to gradually move teeth. Types include metal braces, ceramic braces, lingual braces, and clear aligners like Invisalign. Treatment can take several months to years, aiming to create a healthier bite and better smile.

Tooth Extraction

Price: 8,000Php

A root canal is a dental procedure to save a decayed or infected tooth by removing the damaged pulp, cleaning, disinfecting, and sealing the area. It starts with numbing, then opening the tooth to remove the infected pulp and filling the cleaned space. Finally, the tooth is sealed to restore function.

Flexible Denture

Price: 15,000Php

Flexible dentures, made from a flexible thermoplastic material like nylon or polyester, replace missing teeth comfortably and naturally. They are durable, lightweight, and blend well with the mouth's shape, offering good retention without the need for adhesives or metal clasps. They're ideal for replacing a few missing teeth where aesthetics and comfort matter, but their suitability varies by case and should be discussed with a dentist.

Tooth Whitening

Price: 5,000Php

Tooth whitening is a cosmetic procedure using bleaching agents to lighten teeth. It's done professionally in a dental office or at home with kits recommended by dentists. Whitening isn't permanent and may need touch-ups. Consulting a dentist is important, as it may not be suitable for everyone or certain dental conditions.

Jacket Crown

Price: 6,000Php

A jacket crown, also called a full crown, covers the entire visible part of a tooth and is used for extensive damage. It's custom-made from materials like metal or ceramic, providing strength and a natural appearance. The process involves preparing the tooth, taking an impression, and fitting the crown. They offer durability and aesthetics, lasting for years with proper care.

Tooth Veneer

Price: 26,000Php

A tooth veneer is a thin shell, often porcelain or resin, bonded to the front of a tooth to improve its appearance. It addresses issues like discoloration, chips, gaps, and improves alignment. The process involves consulting with a dentist, preparing the tooth, taking an impression, and bonding the veneer. Veneers are popular for their transformative effect with minimal invasiveness and are durable with proper care.
