About dr. Legaspi

Dr. Kimberly Legaspi, a distinguished dentist, graduated from Centro Escolar University, equipped with a profound understanding of dental care. Hailing from St. Scholastica's Academy in Pampanga, she embarked on a journey to become a beacon of oral health expertise. In her pursuit of excellence, Dr. Legaspi founded Legaspi Dental Clinic on January 12, 2020, a testament to her commitment to providing top-notch dental care. With a passion for transforming smiles and a foundation built on education and experience, Dr. Legaspi has become a trusted name in the field, creating a haven where patients not only receive expert dental services but also experience the warmth of a dental family dedicated to their well-being.
